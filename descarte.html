Santana de Parnaíba has options for disposing of rubble and garbage


Five recycling points and dozens of mobile trash cans installed throughout the city allow residents of Santana de Parnaíba to dispose of rubble and trash properly. This practice contributes to the environment and frees residents from penalties and fines, as the irregular disposal of waste is considered an environmental crime.



Five recycling points and dozens of mobile trash cans installed throughout the city allow residents of Santana de Parnaíba to dispose of rubble and trash properly. This practice contributes to the environment and frees residents from penalties and fines, as the irregular disposal of waste is considered an environmental crime.



Construction waste such as cement, sand, concrete, blocks, bricks, cladding, tiles, etc. should be disposed of in these locations. Organic waste (household waste) should not be thrown into the recycling bins; it can be placed in the various blue mobile bins spread throughout the city. Old furniture (or furniture scraps), leaves and wood are collected by the city government in regular actions under the name of Operação Cata-Treco.
