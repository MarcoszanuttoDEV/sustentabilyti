Santana de Parnaiba leads in
Environment and sustainability in SP


Important Agreement




The City of Santana de Parnaíba and Cetesb (Environmental Company
of the State of São Paulo) signed a historic technical cooperation
agreement for the city. The event took place on Wednesday,
March 19, in the auditorium of the Bandeirantes Administrative
Center, the city hall headquarters, and brought together
hundreds of authorities and representatives of municipalities
in São Paulo. With the agreement, the city assumes licensing ,
inspection and environmental control procedures previously
performed by the State.

According to the agreement, the city government, through SMMAP,
must conduct the licensing process, producing the administrative
acts inherent to its exercise, with the exception of environmental
compensation (this act must be handled by the Environmental
Compensation Chamber of the State of São Paulo). The city
must also submit an Annual Technical Activity Report to Cetesb
on a regular basis and forward, within thirty days of adopting
the act, to the state company the documents that consolidate
the conclusion of the licensing phases and project cycles,
such as licenses and authorizations.

According to the agreement, Cetesb will provide the city government
with copies of administrative processes and environmental
studies eventually presented by the interested party/entrepreneur;
supervise and audit the city government's compliance with its
obligations; notify SMMAP in advance when inspections are
carried out on works, activities and facilities under the licensing
regime; and forward to the city government the administrative acts
produced in the delegation's monitoring process.
