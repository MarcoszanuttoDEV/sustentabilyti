Santana carries out inspections on
official vehicles to avoid pollutants



"Analyzing the smoke emission
of diesel vehicles is a concern
for the environment, because
those that are not in perfect
condition for use will have to
follow the strategic maintenance
plan. " Says an official inspector.

On March 17, 2025, the City of Santana de Parnaíba carried out a technical
inspection of 117 official diesel-powered vehicles, including cars, vans and
buses, in the parking lot of the Municipal Stadium Prefeito Gabriel Marques
da Silva. The objective was to check for black smoke emissions and ensure
that pollutant levels were within environmental standards. Environmental
inspector Bianca Vilar highlighted the importance of the action for proper
vehicle maintenance and environmental preservation. According to the
Municipal Department of Environment and Planning, the annual inspection
also assesses the quality of the air returned to the environment. none of
the inspected vehicles showed irregularities or pollution levels that could
harm the environment or public health.

car inspection for sustainability is an inspection carried out to verify whether a vehicle meets environmental standards, reducing pollutant emissions and promoting cleaner and more efficient transportation.

Car exhaust pollution occurs through the burning of fuel, releasing gases such as carbon monoxide and nitrogen oxides, which are harmful to the environment and health.
