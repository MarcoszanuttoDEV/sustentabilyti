How to Prevent air pollution


Establish limits on pollution levels:

In urban and rural environments, promote constant monitoring of air quality. Santana de Parnaíba has moderate air quality.

Alternative means of transport to cars:

Use of public transport such as the metro and buses and use of transport that does not emit any polluting gases such as bicycles and electric motorcycles, which significantly reduce CO2.

Environmental reforestation:

It balances the climate effects, filters atmospheric pollutants and regulates the flow of water. It contributes to the absorption of CO2, the main greenhouse gas.



How to Prevent soil pollution


Recycling and waste management:

Reusing and recycling waste generated by society.

Constant monitoring:

How to Prevent water pollution


Correct disposal of oil:

Do not throw cooking oil down the sink, separate the oil into bottles and deliver it to cooperatives that transform the oil into soap.

Promote sanitation for the population:

Significantly reduces the amount of waste and pollutants entering water bodies and helps conserve water resources.

Use of biodegradable products

Less chemical residues in the water and they decompose quickly. Use of natural cleaning products or those that do not contain phosphates; biodegradable bags, etc.




Preventing soil, air and water pollution is essential to protect the environment and human health. It helps to:

- Mitigate climate change and global warming
- Protect biodiversity and ecosystems
- Prevent respiratory, cardiovascular and other health conditions

In addition, pollution prevention also contributes to:

- Saving natural resources
- Reducing public health costs
- Improving quality of life

Let's work together for a more sustainable and healthy future by adopting practices such as:

- Using renewable energy
- Reducing waste and recycling
- Using environmentally friendly products



Sustainable ECO-Produts


REUSABLE ECO-BAGS

Reusable shopping bags are a great eco-friendly alternative to disposable plastic bags. They are durable, long-lasting and can be used to reduce the amount of plastic waste.


Recycled paper products are a great way to reduce the amount of trees being cut down. They are made from post-consumer paper and are an eco-friendly alternative to traditional paper products.

PAPER PRODUCTS


BAMBOO PRODUCTS

Bamboo is a fast-growing and sustainable materal that can be used to make a variety of products. Bamboo products are durable, lightweight and look great.
