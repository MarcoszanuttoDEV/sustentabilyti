Santana de Parnaíba carries out action
for environmental recovery on the
Voturuna hill



Five recycling points and dozens of mobile trash cans installed throughout the city allow residents of Santana de Parnaíba to dispose of rubble and trash properly. This practice contributes to the environment and frees residents from penalties and fines, as the irregular disposal of waste is considered an environmental crime.

With the support of a helicopter, the Secretary of the Environment, Veruska Carvalho, accompanied by her team, launched the seeds, reinforcing the municipal administration's commitment to the preservation and recovery of green areas. The action in Voturuna marks another significant step towards revitalizing the local ecosystem, promoting sustainability and environmental balance for future generations.
