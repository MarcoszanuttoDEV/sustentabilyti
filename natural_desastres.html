Natural Disasters



Pirapora do Bom Jesus

(03/23/2015) A foam covered 50 km of the Rio Tiête, in the interior of São Paulo, the most affected city was Pirapora do Bom Jesus, reaching streets and even houses on the margins.

Santana de Parnaíba

(02/10/2020) Rio Tiête almost overflows in Santana de Parnaíba, staying at the same level as the bridge.

Santana de Parnaíba

(03/08/2015) Landslide kills three children and mother was rescued.

Santana de Parnaíba

(12/29/2024) An intense storm hit the city of Santana de Parnaíba with very strong winds, followed by an intense hailstorm.





Osasco

(02/10/2020) Heavy rain causes a landslide in Morro do Socó, in Osasco. There were injuries.

Cajamar

(01/02/2020)The city of Cajamar and region was hit by a strong storm. The Ribeirão dos Cristais River overflowed, causing flooding in several neighborhoods in the district of Jordanésia.

Barueri and Jandira

(09/27/2023) Cities in the Western Region of São Paulo were hit by heavy rain showers. It caused a tree to fall that blocked a road between the cities of Barueri and Jandira.
