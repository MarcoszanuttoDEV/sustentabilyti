7R

The 7 Rs represent seven essential actions for sustainability, each beginning with the letter “R”.
Initially, the concept was based on just 3 Rs (Reduce, Reuse and Recycle), but over time, new sustainable practices were added to encompass a more complete approach to environmental preservation.
